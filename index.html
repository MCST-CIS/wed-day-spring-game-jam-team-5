Game Mechanics

If you have never played before, the goal is simple: Get a hand total closer to 21 than the dealer without going over. If you go over 21, you "bust" and lose the round.

The Card Values: Number Cards (2–10) are worth face value. Face Cards (Jack, Queen, King) are 10. Aces are 1 or 11.
